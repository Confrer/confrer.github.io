Who are we?

Confrere AS is newly founded by two engineers with extensive experience in building products that people love. Our main focus in previous projects has been building communication services that changes the way people interact. And we are now eager to fix a specific aspect of interaction that we think is still broken.

Have you ever had to travel to your doctor, sit in a waiting room, appointment is delayed, and then you spend only 10 minutes with your doctor? We have as well - the feeling of spending 2 hours of our working time just to see the doctor for 10 minutes leaves us with a feeling that there is a problem to fix. We want to tackle this issue, not only in health, but for all professional services that today require a physical meeting. We wish to abolish travelling time, waiting rooms, complex booking systems and radically simplify and improve the situation for both client and professional. We do this by making software that enables professionals to meet clients online, securely, and without the hassle that usually comes with video conference software.
