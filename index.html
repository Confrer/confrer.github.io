Where we're going

Why

Running client meetings online will enable professionals to work more efficiently and spend less resources on physical facilities. Reduced travel and waiting time makes the offering more attractive for a larger number of clients.

How

We are developing software specifically for improving meetings between professionals and clients. It works out of the box, and fits into each professional's website and brand.

What

A complete solution for video consultations between professional and client will solve problems such as client booking, queueing and payment in addition to flawless video meeting experience.
