Why we do what we do

Why

Professionals spend significant resources on maintaining facilities for physical meetings. Clients spend hours not working, sitting in a cramped waiting room, or on the phone trying to book a consultation.

How

Develop software specifically made for improving meetings between professional and clients, that just works out of the box, and fits into each professional's brand.

What

Create a complete solution for scheduling and carrying out video meetings between professional and client, often removing the need to travel to a physical location to have a consultation.
